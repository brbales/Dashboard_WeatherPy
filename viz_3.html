Latitude vs. Cloudiness



This plot provides a clear visualization that there is no discernable relationship between cloud cover and latitude. The lack of any clear trend or similarities to any of the other plots suggest no relationship between cloudiness and the other weather data being examined. This data would be more useful in some kind of timeseries study of weather patterns.
