Project Introduction


The purpose of this project was to prove that weather gets warmer as you approach the equator. In order to do this we were asked compiled and analyzed weather data from hundreds of cities accross the world at varying degrees of latitude.


My approach to this started with randomly generating 750 latitude and longitude coordinate pairs and adding them to a Pandas dataframe. I then ran each coordinate pair through the citipy module to identify the closest city to the random location and adding the associated city and country names to the dataframe. Using the OpenWeatherMap API, I searched by city and country to populate the weather data for temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity into my dataframe. At this step I also replaced the random coordinates in the dataframe with the official coordinates provided by the OpenWeatherMap API in order to ensure that my plots reflect the most accurate data possible.
